## ‚úçÔ∏è Writing Tips

### Do's
- ‚úÖ Use "you" and "we" (conversational)
- ‚úÖ Keep sentences short and clear
- ‚úÖ Focus on benefits, not features
- ‚úÖ Use active voice
- ‚úÖ Include emotional benefits (excitement, relaxation, confidence)
- ‚úÖ Paint a picture of the experience

### Don'ts
- ‚ùå Use technical jargon or buzzwords
- ‚ùå Make unverifiable claims
- ‚ùå Oversell or use excessive hype
- ‚ùå Mention competitors by name
- ‚ùå Get too detailed about how it works
- ‚ùå Use all caps or excessive punctuation!!!

### Power Words
- **Simplicity**: easy, simple, effortless, straightforward
- **Speed**: instant, quick, fast, minutes
- **Quality**: perfect, best, ideal, excellent
- **Personalization**: tailored, customized, your, personalized
- **Confidence**: expert, trusted, reliable, professional
